EPFO Services

What is EPFO and why is it important?

+

EPFO (Employee Provident Fund Organization) manages retirement benefits for employees in India. It's mandatory for most organizations with 20 or more employees. Proper EPFO compliance ensures your employees receive their rightful benefits and keeps your business compliant with labor laws.

How can Emvice help with EPFO compliance?

+

We provide complete EPFO services including registration, monthly return filing, employee registration, UAN generation, transfer claims, withdrawal assistance, and handling EPFO inspections. We manage the entire process so you can focus on running your business.

What documents are needed for EPFO registration?

+

Typically, you'll need business registration documents, PAN card, bank details, employee list with their details, and digital signature. Our team will guide you through the exact requirements based on your specific business situation and ensure all documentation is properly prepared.

Factory Compliance

What is factory compliance and who needs it?

+

Factory compliance involves adhering to the Factories Act and related regulations. Any manufacturing facility with 10 or more workers using power, or 20 or more workers without power, must register under the Factories Act and maintain compliance with safety, health, and environmental standards.

What does your factory compliance service include?

+

Our factory compliance services include factory license registration and renewal, Factory Act compliance management, safety and health regulations adherence, environmental compliance, fire safety protocols, workplace safety audits, machinery certifications, and regular compliance monitoring.

How often do factory inspections occur?

+

Factory inspections can be conducted by authorities at any time, though routine inspections typically occur annually or semi-annually. We help you maintain inspection-ready compliance year-round, so you're always prepared for both scheduled and surprise inspections.

Labor Law & Statutory Compliance

What labor laws do businesses need to comply with?

+

Key labor laws include the Payment of Wages Act, Minimum Wages Act, ESI Act, Gratuity Act, Bonus Act, Maternity Benefits Act, and various state-specific Shops and Establishment Acts. The specific laws applicable to your business depend on factors like industry, location, and number of employees.

What is statutory compliance?

+

Statutory compliance refers to the legal framework within which organizations must operate. This includes compliance with tax laws (GST, TDS, Income Tax), Professional Tax, maintaining statutory registers, filing annual returns, and adhering to various central and state regulations applicable to your business.

What happens if my business is non-compliant?

+

Non-compliance can result in penalties, fines, legal notices, and in severe cases, imprisonment of responsible persons. It can also damage your business reputation and affect employee morale. Our proactive compliance management helps you avoid these risks entirely.

Do you handle compliance for remote teams?

+

Yes, we handle compliance for businesses with remote teams, including multi-state operations. We ensure compliance with regulations specific to each location where your employees work, managing the complexity of different state laws and requirements.
